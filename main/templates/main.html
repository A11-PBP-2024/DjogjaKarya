Introducing DjogjaKarya

From DjogjaKarya serves as a bridge connecting Yogyakarta's cultural heritage with the digital realm. Inspired by the word Djogja symbolizing uniqueness and strength, and Karya representing the creations of artisans, DjogjaKarya is a platform dedicated to marketing Yogyakarta's handcrafted products to a broader audience. With product categories that include batik, leather crafts, Kotagede silver, wayang crafts, wooden crafts, woven items, Kasongan pottery, lurik woven fabric, and bamboo products, DjogjaKarya is committed to being a venue for local artisans to showcase their works to the wider community.
